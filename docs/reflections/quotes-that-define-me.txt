Quotes and sentences that define the way I think

15th July 2025

This essay contains sentences and quotes that define the way I think. Some of them are sentences that I say very often that my friends will think about me if they hear them from someone else. All of them have a context and a personal explanation behind, so if you want to know about it, just reach out and I'll be more than happy to share my thoughts.

- GO BIG OR GO HOME - Silicon Valley

- The biggest risk in your life is not taking any risks at all

- The word impossible shouldn't exist

- It's equally or more important to know what you don't want to do with your life than knowing what to do.

- "Live in the future and build what seems interesting" - Paul Graham

- Nothing is as good or as bad as it seems

- "Don't save what's left after spending, instead spend what's left after saving" - Warren Buffet

- "MAKE IT HAPPEN" - REVER

- "Full gas we are never done" - REVER

- "Nothing in this world can take the place of persistence. Talent will not: nothing is more common than unsuccessful men with talent. Genius will not; unrewarded genius is almost a proverb. Education will not; the world is full of educated fools. Persistence and determination alone are omnipotent." - Ray Kroc 

- "Focus on the road, not the wall" - Ben Horowitz
